# Welcome to Jus'Study!
Jus'Study is a YSWS program, sponsored by `@jps` at [Hack Club](https://hackclub.com).
This is a program where you 'ship' a study-related project and we ship you prizes for it!
This page has all of the information you need to get started.

## What could I make?
The best way for you to build something you will actually work on and not abandon is to make something that **you** personally want. Think of a problem which you have that **you** want to solve, and code/make that.
It's what's seperated my projects from something that I think would be cool but then just abandon and something that can actually build my skills and will actually make a difference to my workflow and my life.

Saying that, It's normal to not know what to make, so here are some ideas to get you started:

### Software
- A pomorodo timer
- A flashcard app
- A way to gamify your studying
- An app to lock down social media apps when studying

### Hardware
- A cable management clip
- A timer for studying
- A desk dashboard with assessments coming up

## What do I get?
Depending on what sort of project you build, you could get:
### Hardware
We will send you a grant to build your project. Where you can then log the time spent building it to get prizes!
### Software
You will log your time spent building and get to choose prizes from the shop related to study!
