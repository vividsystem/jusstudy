## Jus'STUDY FAQ
This is our FAQ for the Jus'Study YSWS. Make sure to read through this before you submit a support ticket or DM one of us.

### I don't know how to code, can I still make a project?
Yes! We welcome beginners to make projects, and you can go through our guides or if you need any help feel free to ask on the slack!

### What types of projects can I make?
You can only make the following types of core projects:
- Only Hardware (Schematic, PCB, etc.)
- Only Software (Python app, Website, Game)
- Only CAD (Must be just CAD)

A 'core project' is the part of the project where you have invested the most of your time.

You CAN however make side assets, which are other things you do but not inside the core project.

For example, if you have invested 10 hours on a project, and 8 hours of that was spent on making the schematic and pcb of a calculator, but 2 hours of it was making a case with CAD, then the core project is hardware.

Note that any time spent on making side assets should NOT exceed 25% of the total time spent on the project.

Finally:
- The Project **must** be somehow RELATED TO STUDY. (Ex. Custom Calculator, Study Monitoring website, etc.)
- The Project should be made with the motive of LEARNING SOMETHING NEW, or making something REALLY AWESOME that NO ONE EVER MADE BEFORE.
- If you have any doubt if your project is related to the theme or not, and if you can make that project for this YSWS, in that case feel free to ask the question on #jus-study-help

### SHIPPING/TIME
All of your projects must be tracked using Hackatime. You also need a minimum of 2 hours of work on any project before shipping it. You must sync your lapse projects to Hackatime.


### How do I get paid?

- For Projects UNDER 10 HOURS, You will only get 10 PAGES/HOUR max.
- For Projects OVER 10 HOURS, You can get up to 13 PAGES/HOUR Max. based on the voting done by ppl
- The Min. payout will be 7 PAGES/HOUR according to voting.
- EXCEPTIONS: If we find any project that is VERY low quality/ made just for the purpose of getting the payout, we can even give that project a payout LESS THAN 7 PAGES/HOUR or 0 payout in very exceptional cases! (This Point is applicable to the rarest of projects)
- If you are making any hardware project that needs irl parts to be built, you will get a grant to buy it and IT WOULD BE CUT FROM YOUR PAYOUT IN THE FORM OF THIS YSWS's CURRENCY!


### What requirements do I need in my project?

- There must be a README.md in your Github Repository with most of the info abt your project, not in much detail, but kinda a whole overview of what u made.
- There must be a DEMO of your project where anyone can see and have a look at what u made. (for hardware a video)
- Your project must be MADE BY YOURSELF with any code/text/art from others credited and not straight copy-pasted.
- MINIMUM 2 HOURS required.

### Project examples
- Someone makes a calculator using hardware with a total of 8 hours of hardware (80% of whole), and 2 hours of CAD (20% of whole), totaling to 10 hours! but a few pages/books (currency) from payout would be cut for the hardware components to be bought.
- A cool guy makes a cool website which greets everyone with a cool text and cool UI. He spends 15 Hours on building the Web, and 3 Hours on a Cool Art on Figma. He gets the full payout, Hooray!!!
- Someone builds a pen holder using complete CAD, which is 10 hour of only CAD! This works too!
